Site Plan for Crowdly Media

Site Name

Crowdly Media

I chose the name Crowdly Media because it is the name of my creative marketing agency that I just started and I want to bring it to life in a sense through this and practice for my actual agency site. The name represents the fact that we help companies and businesses reach and target their right crowd. The domain name is crowdlymedia.co.za.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to showcase Crowdly Media's portfolio, services, team, and client testimonials. It will also provide information on how to contact and engage with Crowdly Media.

Scenarios

1. What creative services and solutions does Crowdly Media offer for digital marketing?

2. I would like to see Crowdly's past works and what they can do.
