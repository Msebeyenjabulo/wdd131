Crowdly Media Site Plan

Site Name

Crowdly Media

I chose the name Crowdly Media because it is the name of my creative marketing agency that I just started. I want to bring it to life in a sense through this project and practice for my actual agency site. The name represents the fact that we help companies and businesses reach and target their right crowd. The domain name is crowdlymedia.co.za.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to showcase Crowdly Media's portfolio, services, team, and client testimonials. It will also provide information on how to contact and engage with Crowdly Media.

Scenarios

What creative services and solutions does Crowdly Media offer for digital marketing?

I would like to see Crowdly Media's past works and what they can do.

Color Scheme

Primary Colors:

  • Dark Grey: #171717
  • Light Grey: #d1d1d1

Accent Color:

  • Black

The overall design will be minimal, sleek, and modern to keep the website light and let the portfolio work stand out.

Typography

Logo Font: Lot

Heading Font: Intro

Body Font: Red Hat Display

The heading font is a thick and chunky font similar to the logo font, while the body font is lighter and pairs well for readability.
